Figure 7. Pre-edit monitor. For each of the 49 tasks, we hold the task out, compute v_noop on the OTHER 48 tasks' mean-difference at L24 / pos −1, and project the held-out task's BUGGY and FIXED residuals onto that unit vector. Negated projection is the classifier score (lower raw projection = more buggy-like = predicted-edit). Under LOOCV the projection is fully out-of-sample. ROC-AUC = 1.000; AP = 1.000. At the operating point (red dot, max balanced accuracy), precision = 1.00, recall = 1.00, accuracy = 1.00, false-edit rate (fixed prompts misclassified as buggy) = 0.00. The monitor labels are the GROUND TRUTH `buggy vs fixed` condition (i.e. the action a correct agent should take), not the model's overt action choice — which is the relevant target for a pre-edit safety system.
