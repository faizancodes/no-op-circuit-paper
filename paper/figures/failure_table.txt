Figure 5. Failure analysis under stale evidence (Qwen, N=49 per variant). Left: argmax-action distribution. Under both `stale_misleading` (fixed code + passing tests + misleading issue text) and `stale_flaky` (same + one unrelated synthetic flaky failure), the model overwhelmingly defers to `grep` (>90%) and explicitly chooses `noop` 0% of the time. The over-edit failure mode is mild (6–8%). Right: distribution of residual projections onto v_noop. The stale-variant distributions sit *between* the clean-buggy (mean −5.53) and clean-fixed (mean +0.36) baselines: the model internally registers the test-passing signal, but routes uncertainty to information-gathering rather than abstention.
