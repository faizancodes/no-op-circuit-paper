Figure 3. SAE decomposition of v_noop on Qwen (d_sae=4096, k=16, EV=0.976). (A) Cumulative OMP top-k ablation: top-1 alone is ineffective, top-2 reaches +26.4%, top-3 hits the +34% plateau; the minimum sufficient subset is 3 features, not 8. (B) Argmax-action distribution under clean vs OMP top-8 ablation. The model's clean default is `grep` (≈84%); ablating the 8 features flips the argmax on 80.1% of prompts and every flip is `grep→edit`, collapsing the action distribution onto `edit` on both buggy and fixed prompts. This is behavioural override, not calibration. The same procedure on CodeGemma yields +6.4% reduction (p=0.10, not significant) — see Appendix H.
